📜 League Constitution & Rules

1. Buy-In & Financials

  • Annual Buy-In: $100 per manager. All dues must be paid via LeagueSafe prior to the draft.
  • Payout Structure:
    Placement Payout Amount
    🏆 League Champion (1st) $700
    🥈 Runner-Up (2nd) $200
    🥉 Third Place (3rd) $100
    👑 Regular Season Points King $200

2. Roster Settings & Lineups

  • Starting Lineup Slots: 1 QB, 2 RB, 2 WR, 1 TE, 2 FLEX (W/R/T), 1 K, 1 DEF.
  • Bench & Reserve Capacity: 6 Bench slots, 2 Injured Reserve (IR) slots.
  • IR Eligibility: Players must have an official 'Out' (O) or 'Injured Reserve' (IR) designation on Sleeper to occupy an IR slot.

3. Keeper Policy Summary

  • First-Year Keepers: Cost matches the draft round from the previous season.
  • Free Agents & Waivers: Waiver wires and FA pickups default to a 14th-round cost.
  • Second-Year Keepers: Round 1 selections are ineligible. Rounds 2-3 cost minus one round. Rounds 4-14 and FAs automatically jump to a flat 3rd-round cost.
  • Maximum Caps: No player can be kept more than two consecutive seasons (3 years total roster locks).
