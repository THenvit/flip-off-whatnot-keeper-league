🏈 Flip Off Whatnot - League Rules & Scoring

12-Team Sleeper League | Full PPR | Snake Draft

Buy-In & Financials

  • Annual Buy-In: $100 per manager. All dues must be paid via LeagueSafe prior to the draft.
  • Payout Structure:
    Placement Payout Amount
    🏆 League Champion (1st) $700
    🥈 Runner-Up (2nd) $200
    🥉 Third Place (3rd) $100
    👑 Regular Season Points King $200

League Overview

  • Platform: Sleeper
  • Teams: 12
  • Scoring: Full PPR
  • Draft Format: Snake Draft

Keeper Structure

Each team may keep:
  • 1 Rookie Keeper
  • 1 Open Keeper (any eligible player)

Managers may keep fewer than two players but may not exceed two keepers.

Keeper Eligibility

Rookie Keeper

Must have entered the NFL as a rookie during the previous season and be on the manager's roster at season end.

Open Keeper

Any eligible player on a manager's final roster may be selected.

Keeper Cost

A keeper costs the draft round in which the player was originally selected.

Example:

  • Drafted Round 8
  • Kept Next Season = Round 8 Cost

Keeper Escalation

Players may be kept for a maximum of two consecutive seasons.

The additional keeper season costs are calculated based on original round drafted as follows:

  • Round 4 to Round 14 or Free Agent/Waiver: Automatic 3rd Round Pick
  • Round 3: 2nd Round Pick
  • Round 2: 1st Round Pick
  • Round 1: Ineligible Keeper

Example:

  • 2026 Draft: Round 10
  • 2027 Keeper: Round 10
  • 2028 Keeper: Round 9
  • Returns to draft pool in 2029

Misc. Keeper Rules

Two Keepers with same draft round cost.

If both your keepers would have the same Keeper Cost, the cost for the second would be one round earlier than original cost:

Example:

  • Keeping Player A for the 2nd time: Costs 3rd Round Pick
  • Keeping Player B from previous year 3rd round pick: Costs 3rd Round Pick
  • Cost to keep both players: 2nd Round and 3rd round pick
  • Note: If both players would cost a 1st round pick you may not keep both.

Trades

  • Keeper rights transfer with the player.
  • Keeper value remains unchanged after a trade.
  • Keeper history also transfers.
  • Trades may not be used to reset keeper eligibility.

Waivers & Free Agents

Waiver and free-agent pickups receive a keeper value equal to the final draft round.

Example: An undrafted player picked up during the season can be kept using the manager's last-round draft pick.

Keeper Deadline

All keeper selections must be submitted one week before the annual draft.

Competitive Integrity

  • No collusion.
  • No intentional tanking.
  • Managers must set active lineups.
  • Commissioner reserves the right to review suspicious activity.

Rule Changes

  • Standard rule changes require majority approval.
  • Keeper rule changes require a two-thirds majority.
  • Rule changes are not retroactive unless unanimously approved.
